MISSIONED SOULS: A Family United by Faith, Music, and Joy

MISSIONED SOULS is more than just a band; they are a vibrant family unit on a heartfelt mission. Formed in 2022, this talented family of six brings a unique blend of faith, love, and musical passion to every performance, playing a diverse repertoire of pop and oldies hits that resonate with audiences of all ages.

At the core of MISSIONED SOULS is a profound commitment to propagating the importance of family unity. They believe in demonstrating love and respect to each family member and for one another, embodying these values in their lives and their music. Their sound is a joyful outpouring of God-given gifts, put into action to bring happiness and fun into other people's lives.

The band was born out of an earnest desire to give thanks for the simple yet profound musical talent they have received from above, thus dedicating their efforts to the glory of the Holy Trinity. As they often say, "Praise God and Hail Mary!"
